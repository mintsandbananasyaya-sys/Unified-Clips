How Unified Clips Works

A quick guide to browsing, watching, and uploading clips from across the Civ Union — plus the ground rules that keep the feed worth scrolling.

Getting Started

No account is required to watch. Uploading just needs a video file and a few details — login comes later.

Categories

What each tag means

Every clip gets filed under one category. Here's how to pick the right one when you upload.

PvP

Direct fights — raids, sieges, duels, last-stands. Anything decided with weapons.

Diplomacy

Alliances, votes, betrayals, leaked deals — the political side of a season.

Build

Builds, reveals, and construction moments worth showing off on their own merit.

Chaos

Everything that doesn't fit cleanly elsewhere — the unplanned, the chaotic, the funny.

Before You Upload

Content guidelines

These keep the feed made of real moments from real events, not edited filler.

✓

Clips from official Civ Union events

Footage should come from an actual event run by one of the linked civilizations — not a private session or unaffiliated server.

✓

Raw gameplay

Upload the moment as it happened. No edited compilations and no music overlays — the feed is about what occurred, not a highlight reel production.

✓

Descriptive titles

Say what happened, not who won. A title like "Copperdale sneaks a midnight raid past two watchtowers" tells viewers what they're about to watch.

✕

Misleading titles get removed

Titles written to bait clicks or misrepresent the outcome of a clip will be taken down on review.
